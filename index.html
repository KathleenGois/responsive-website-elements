Image Layout

— Grid gallery

The photos are displayed in an irregular grid (some photos span over multiple columns or rows). After you click on the photo, it will be displayed in a lightbox in the biggest possible size—depending on the viewport.

Inside the lightbox, you can navigate to next or previous photographs that are in an infinite loop. You can do it using mouse or keyboard. To close the lightbox, press Escape key, click the closing icon or simply click outside of the image. Lightbox feature is written in pure JavaScript.

— Gallery slider

Thi is a simple image gallery slider where you can go to the next or previous image using arrows that appear next to the current photo. You can also go quickly to a chosen image by choosing a dot representing it under the current photo.
